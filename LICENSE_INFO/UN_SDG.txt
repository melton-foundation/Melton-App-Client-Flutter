The United Nations' 17 Sustainable Development Goals were adopted in 2015.
The UN SDG logos have been used in this app for informational purposes only. 
They are used to represent issues that Fellows are passionate about and actively work in or have worked in the past.

For more details refer: https://www.un.org/sustainabledevelopment/wp-content/uploads/2019/01/SDG_Guidelines_January_2019.pdf
